Welcome to our Ada's Kitchen

Our Team


Ada's Kitchen is an online restaurant that handles all your food needs both indoor & outdoor. Our sumptuous meals are produced under a very stringent hygienic environment to cater for different customer experience.

This idea was born out of passion for cooking and transformed into a small scale venture not meant for one person alone but for friends & family to enjoy as a whole. In the quest to actualize the idea, Ada's Kitchen was born.

Over time, the business has received recognition from friends from far & wide which prompted the outdoor catering services at weddings, conferences & other events.

With her expertise, this business has been meeting the needs of young and old, even to the extent of training other people in perfecting their culinary skills.
